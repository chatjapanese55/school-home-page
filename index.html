FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior Japanese knowledge to join?

No prior knowledge is required! Our courses are designed for complete beginners as well as those with some basic Japanese. We tailor lessons to your current level.

What is the minimum course duration?

While 1-week courses are available, we recommend a minimum of 2 weeks to truly unlock daily conversation skills and feel comfortable living in Japan.

How many students are in each class?

We maintain a small class size of up to 8 students maximum to ensure personalized attention and maximum speaking practice for every participant.

Where do the lessons take place?

Lessons take place in various locations around Tokyo — specialty cafes, museums, markets, and art galleries. We believe the best classroom is Tokyo itself!
